## 2. Services

My work centers on **retreat storytelling** — capturing the feeling, people, atmosphere, rhythm, and transformation of a retreat experience so the content can be used for future launches, social media, brand storytelling, community-building, and long-term retreat promotion.

Depending on the Proposal, services may include:

- Multi-day retreat photography and videography
- Cinematic retreat highlight films
- Social media reels and short-form clips
- Daily recap videos or summary reels
- Facilitator, host, instructor, and speaker portraits
- Documentary coverage of classes, workshops, ceremonies, rituals, meals, group moments, conversations, and shared experiences
- Venue, landscape, atmosphere, and detail content
- Behind-the-scenes footage
- Participant testimonial clips, where planned and consented to in advance
- Promotional storytelling content for future retreat launches
- Content designed for website, email marketing, social media, portfolio, and organic brand visibility

My style is **documentary, cinematic, and unobtrusive**. I capture what naturally unfolds rather than staging or over-directing the experience. I may offer light direction where useful, especially for portraits, host moments, brand storytelling, promotional shots, or intentional visual sequences.

These services are especially suited to:

- Yoga retreats
- Wellness retreats
- Breathwork, somatic, meditation, and embodiment experiences
- Women's retreats and transformational gatherings
- Boutique travel and destination retreats
- Spiritual, creative, and educational multi-day experiences
- Facilitator-led group programs
- Retreats where atmosphere, emotion, trust, and lived experience matter as much as the formal schedule

The following are **not offered** unless expressly agreed in writing:

- Studio product photography
- E-commerce photography
- Heavily staged commercial production
- Architectural or interior design photography
- Advanced beauty retouching
- Complex visual effects or compositing
- Large-scale agency production services

Only Deliverables listed in the Proposal are included. Additional services, edits, formats, shoot days, usage rights, or Deliverables requested after acceptance may be quoted separately.

- Travel, accommodation, venue, equipment, subcontractor, or crew costs already booked for the Project may be charged.
- Cancellations close to the retreat date may carry additional fees if stated in the Proposal.

Rescheduling requires at least **24 hours' notice** and is subject to availability.

If a Project is rescheduled, the existing Retainer may be transferred to the new date at my discretion, provided the new date is available and the rescheduling request is reasonable.

If I need to reschedule due to illness, emergency, technical failure, travel disruption, or another force majeure event, the Project will be rescheduled at no additional creative fee, and any Retainer already paid will remain valid for the new date.

Force majeure includes circumstances beyond reasonable control, including but not limited to illness, injury, severe weather, natural disaster, transport disruption, government restrictions, venue closure, civil unrest, equipment theft, power failure, or other events that make performance unsafe, impossible, or materially impractical.

Weather conditions may require adapting the creative approach. Weather does not constitute a breach of this Agreement.
